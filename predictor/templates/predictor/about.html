Justification

Why these technical choices were made.

Efficient baseline

EfficientNet-B0 gives strong results quickly and is ideal for a prototype.

Practical UX

Urgency + contagious flag + self-care + red-flags make results easier to understand.

Faster Training

Because of Hardware limitations the AI was trained on a more efficient model

Note

This is not medical advice and should be used for educational / triage support only.
